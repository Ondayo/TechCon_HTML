History

TechCon began as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts in 2000. Over the years, it has grown into one of the largest technology conferences in the world, attracting innovators and thought leaders from various industries.

Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation, inspire collaboration, and advance the frontiers of technology. TechCon 2024 aims to bring together the brightest minds to explore groundbreaking ideas and create a better future.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted a remarkable lineup of speakers who have shaped the tech industry:

  • Ada Lovelace: A pioneer in computing and the world's first programmer.
  • Alan Turing: Known as the father of modern computing and AI.
  • Grace Hopper: An innovator in programming languages and a trailblazer for women in tech.
